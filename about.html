A Letter

Words He'd Never Say Out Loud

August 5, 2021 — and every day since
Dear Jahra,

I didn't know what was happening the day you grabbed my clothes. I just know that something shifted. Like a door swung open in a house I'd been living in my whole life, and suddenly there was a room I didn't know existed — warm and bright and completely yours.

You are the most unexpected thing that's ever happened to me. I don't say this lightly. I've chased plans, built goals, mapped out futures — and none of it accounted for you. You arrived like a plot twist in a story I thought I already knew the ending to. And now I can't imagine the story without you in it.

On the days I forget to say it — and I know there are too many of those — know that it's there. Constant. Quiet. Like breathing. You make everything feel possible. You make me want to be the kind of man who deserves someone like you.

With everything I have and everything I'm still becoming,
Ilham
